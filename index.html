Mobile AI-Enabled Vehicle Assistant System

Intelligent vehicle diagnosis, real-time emergency detection, damage assessment, and smart service recommendations in one powerful mobile app.

Key Features

Comprehensive vehicle assistance powered by artificial intelligence and sensor fusion

📊

Dashboard Diagnosis

AI-powered dashboard warning light recognition. Capture a photo and get instant explanations of vehicle issues.

💰

Cost Estimation

Accurate repair cost predictions using machine learning. Get realistic estimates before visiting a garage.

📍

Smart Locator

Location-based repair center recommendations with ratings, availability, and real-time towing activation.

🚨

Crash Detection

Multi-sensor emergency detection with automatic emergency contact notification and digital accident reports.

🔒

Secure & Private

End-to-end encryption with secure cloud storage. Your vehicle data stays protected and private.

⚡

Real-time Response

Instant notifications to hospitals, ambulances, police, and family members in emergency situations.

System Modules

Four integrated AI modules working together for comprehensive vehicle support

1

Dashboard Scanner

Computer vision-based warning light classification using CNN models to identify vehicle issues from dashboard images.

  • EfficientNetB0 & VGG16 models
  • 11 warning categories
  • 99%+ accuracy
  • Real-time classification
2

Cost Estimator

ML-based repair cost prediction combining vehicle specs, damage info, and localized pricing data from Sri Lankan repair market.

  • Random Forest regression
  • Localized pricing
  • Cost breakdown
  • Insurance integration
3

Smart Locator

GPS-based service provider recommendations with bidding system, ratings, and real-time towing service activation.

  • Location-based filtering
  • Service ratings
  • Quote comparison
  • Towing activation
4

Crash Detector

Multi-sensor decision-level fusion combining audio, OBD-II, IMU, and GPS for accurate accident detection and emergency response.

  • Multi-modal fusion
  • Audio + MFCC features
  • Automated alerting
  • Digital accident reports
