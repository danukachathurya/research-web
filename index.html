Research Domain

Domain Overview

The RoadResq research domain focuses on safer, faster, and smarter post-incident and maintenance support for vehicle owners through AI, computer vision, sensor fusion, and mobile computing.

Research Problem

Drivers often struggle to interpret dashboard warnings, estimate repair costs, identify reliable nearby service providers, and react quickly during accidents. Existing tools usually solve these as separate problems and rarely provide one connected, localized, mobile-friendly system.

Research Gap

There is limited integration of vehicle diagnostics, cost estimation, repair center discovery, and emergency response in a single AI-assisted ecosystem designed for Sri Lankan vehicle users and local service conditions.

01

Computer Vision Diagnostics

Studies show CNN-based classification can identify dashboard warning categories with high accuracy from captured images.

02

Predictive Cost Modeling

Regression and ensemble models can estimate repair cost when vehicle condition, damage type, and market pricing are known.

03

Emergency Detection

Multi-sensor accident detection improves reliability by combining IMU, audio, location, and vehicle telemetry signals.

Expected Research Impact

RoadResq aims to reduce confusion around vehicle issues, improve response speed during emergencies, help users make better repair decisions, and strengthen trust between drivers and local service providers through explainable AI support.
